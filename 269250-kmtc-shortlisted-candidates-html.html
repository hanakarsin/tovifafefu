    The Kenya Medical Training College is a state corporation established in 1927 by an Act of Parliament Cap 261 of the Laws of Kenya. The institution's mandate is to train health professionals in Kenya. Therefore, KMTC offers a variety of certificate, diploma and post-basic diploma courses. And since the learning institution is accredited by the Ministry of Education and is under the Ministry of Health, graduates are easily absorbed in the health industry in various capacities.

    Every year, the Kenya Medical Training College (KMTC) invites interested applicants to join the learning institution to undertake certificate, diploma, and higher diploma courses. The institution has been, for long, regarded as the ultimate centre for clinical studies, more so nursing.

    If you have been longing to join the college and have applied for the available KMTC courses, you are likely wondering what next. So, how does one apply and confirm if they have been accepted to study at the institution?

    KMTC shortlisted candidates for the 2024 intake

    According to the past announcement, all successful candidates were to join the prestigious learning institution in the September 2023 intake and March 2024 intake.

    So, are you looking to join the college in March? Have you confirmed your admission status? Are you trying to figure out how to check whether you were admitted to The Kenya Medical Training College?

    According to the KTMC’s Deputy Director, Administration and Finance, Dr. Kelly Oluoch, the school will be issuing admission letters from the first week of January. In a video, he advised prospective students to be on the lookout for their respective admission letters to learn if they have been admitted to the medical training institute.

    You can confirm whether you are among the KMTC 2024 shortlisted candidates by following the below procedure. The best thing is that everything is online, and you don't have to visit the learning institution physically.

    How to apply for KMTC courses

    Only those who applied through the college's admission portal and met the requirements are the KTMC 2024 shortlisted candidates. The college ordinarily advertises available courses and advises students to use the online platform, making the process relatively straightforward. So, how does one apply for KMTC programs?

    Procedure for creating an account at the KMTC admission portal

    Before going to the official KTMC website, you need to ascertain that you possess a valid email address. It is advisable to create or have one that you can recall and access. Once you are ready with your email address, follow the following procedure to create an account for preservice and in-service applicants:

    • Log to KMTC website www.kmtc.ac.ke.
    • On the menu, click on the admission portal menu item, the second-last item on the left side.
    • You will land on the online registration portal.
    • If you have registered an account, log in using your email and password.
    • If you don’t have an account, click on register in the top right section of the website.
    • This will land you on another page with empty slots requiring more information.
    • Enter your valid email and password.
    • Click on the Register button.
    • An email will be sent to your email address with a link to verify your account.
    • Open your email and click on the email link.
    • You will be redirected to the KMTC portal.
    • Fill in your personal information on the account creation form provided.
    • Click on the register button.
    • You will be provided with a form to confirm your data.
    • Check information accuracy
    • and click save changes.

    KMTC courses application process

    The application process is the most sensitive as it is the only way that you are going to join the college for the desired medical course. Therefore, you must fill in all information accurately and carefully follow all instructions. You can use the following steps to apply for the desired course at KMTC.

    • On the left sidebar, click on the applications.
    • From the drop-down menu, click Apply.
    • You will be directed to option 1-course selection.
    • Select the Option 1-course level (Diploma or Certificate) and click Search. A form will appear on the page.
    • Select your desired course.
    • Fill in the form details as guided by the labels and ensure that all fields are filled.
    • Click on Submit Application.
    • You will be redirected to Option-2 Form.
    • Select the course level (Diploma or Certificate) and click Search. The actions will bring a form on the page.
    • Select your desired Option 2-course.
    • Fill in the form details as guided by the labels and click on Submit Application.
    • You will be redirected to the Upload KCSE certificate page.
    • Select the KCSE certificate/result slip soft-copy file and click Upload.
    • You will be redirected to the upload leaving certificate page.
    • Select the leaving certificate soft-copy file and click Upload.
    • You will be redirected to the upload National ID/Birth Certificate/Passport page.
    • Select the leaving National ID/Birth Certificate/Passport soft-copy file on the front side.
    • Select the leaving National ID/ Birth Certificate/ Passport soft-copy file on the backside and click Upload.
    • You will be redirected to confirm courses page.
    • Make sure the courses listed reflect your selected courses.
    • If you wish to change the course, click on the Reject button.
    • You will be redirected to the application page again.
    • Once satisfied, click confirm, and you will be redirected to make a payment of Ksh.2,022 to MPESA Paybill number 964150 (Confirm that the payment is paid to Kenya Medical Training College).
    • Click on Confirm button.
    • If you get a message stating, "Please pay Ksh.2022 to Paybill number 96150 To Confirm the Courses", wait for up to ten minutes and refresh the page.
    • A confirmation SMS will be sent to your phone number (Added as phone number 1 while entering personal information).
    • If you don't get an SMS, please call the following numbers for assistance – 0723004516, 0723000429, 0736212060, 0736993813
    • Click on My Applications from the left menu to see your applied courses.

    How do you check your KMTC application status?

    The best way to confirm your application status is by visiting the official KMTC admission portal. You can access the online platform from the college's website.

    How do you check your KMTC balance?

    You can check your balance through the KMTC student portal. And you can do it using the following steps:

    • Navigate to the Student Portal or visit portal.kmtc.ac.ke.
    • Enter your Student Admission No./Employee Number and Password to log in.
    • Click on Fees.
    • Click on the statement of fees to view the breakdown and total fees to be paid.
    • If you wish to pay your fees, click on the tab to pay the fees.
    • Follow the steps gradually to pay the fees at designated banks.

    The Kenya Medical Training College allows students to pay their school fees in instalments. Unfortunately, most students forget their 2nd instalment and must confirm their balance with the learning institution.

    How many intakes does KMTC have?

    The Kenya Medical Training College has two official intakes in September and March every year. The college always advertises available courses for these intake periods for in-service and preservice students who want to join the institution.

    The Kenya Medical Training College has various campuses all over Kenya, which admit students every year in its two intakes (September and March). Therefore, any of the KMTC shortlisted candidates are admitted to these campuses for the desired courses.
