    Nigerian woman settles brawl by doing THIS, embarrasses the country (photo, video)

    - A Nigerian woman in Delhi metro settled a dispute in an unusual way - Two women got into a fight with some male passengers over a seat and things degenerated - The moment was captured on a video

    24 Mar 2024, 00:00

    - A Nigerian woman in Delhi metro settled a dispute in an ‘unusual’ way

    - Two women got into a fight with some male passengers over a seat and things degenerated

    - The moment was captured on a video

    Following the attacks launched on some African students in Greater Noida some weeks ago, another incident of alleged racism against blacks has occurred.

    In this incident, two African women got into a fight with some male passengers over a seat. One of the women stepped in to save the other woman who was being verbally abused by the other passengers.

    The Nigerian woman wearing a sweat shirt stepped in to save the moment. She settled the fight the Afro way which was rather unusual to the other passengers.

    She stripped herself of her clothes and dared the passengers to fight her since they wanted to fight. That was her way of dousing the burning issue as a man was heard saying ‘Don’t do this’ as soon as she took her top off.
