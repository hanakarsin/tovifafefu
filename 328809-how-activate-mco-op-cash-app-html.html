    Technology in banking has already transformed the financial sector. Nowadays, you don’t have to visit any bank to do your banking or transactions. You can do them on your gadget at the comfort of your seat, while swimming, or even walking to your workplace. Cooperative Bank of Kenya hasn’t been left behind either as they have introduced MCo-op cash for their customers. How can you enrol in this service? What are the requirements? Do you know how to activate MCo-op cash app?

    Making time for visiting the bank for transactions can sometimes be tough, especially when you are busy during day time. However, this should never inconvenience you as you can bank anytime and anywhere with M-Coop cash app. Find out how you can enrol and activate your Cooperative Bank app and much more below.

    What is MCo-op cash?

    Before going straight to the procedural part, it is imperative to understand what this service is. MCo-op cash is a Cooperative Bank of Kenya initiative that lets customers do banking on their mobile devices through USSD or app.

    To access the service, you have to dial *667# or install the application on your gadget. With this, you can do 14 things on your device without having to visit the bank as follows:

  • Save or deposit money
  • Access or pay Cooperative Bank of Kenya loans
  • Send money to a SACCO account, another Co-op Bank account, or an MCo-op cash account.
  • Withdraw funds at any Co-operative Bank ATM or MCo-op agent
  • Transfer any amount from your Co-op or SACCO account.
  • Buy airtime for any subscriber countrywide.
  • Pay bills like DSTV, KRA, KPLC and so on
  • Shell out any amount for goods and services offered
  • Sell, buy and transfer shares
  • Check your account balance
  • Get a statement
  • Pay rent, school fees and so on
  • SMS alerts for credits and debits
  • Send money from your account to your mobile wallet such as M-Pesa

    • MCo-op cash application

    To use this service, you must visit any Co-operative Bank of Kenya branch near you. You will need your national ID number and account number to link your account to your mobile number.

    An activation OTP PIN code will be sent via SMS. To activate your MCo-op cash, you can either dial *667# or use a mobile application. If you decide to use the USSD code, you should have at least have some airtime balance.

    You will need the following for activation via an app:

    • Active internet connection,
    • Enough space on your gadget to accommodate a new app,
    • Unrooted/not jailbroken phone: MCo-op cash does not support rooted devices.
    • The OTP PIN code sent to you

    How to activate MCo-op cash app on Android

    Android is one of the most popular Operating Software (OS) in the country. Most of the people own smartphones operating on this OS. Therefore, it would be appalling for the Cooperative Bank of Kenya to leave behind these users on their latest technology upgrade.

    Use the following steps to install and activate your MCoop cash loans app on Android:

  • Connect to your Internet Service Provider (ISP): You can use any trusted, fast Wi-Fi. Alternatively, you could purchase data bundles then switch on your internet connection.
  • Allow background data: Some processes will require internet connections during operation on your gadget’s background.
  • Open the Google “Play Store”: The app is visible on your home screen. If not, go to your app list scroll until you locate the application. Click on its emblem to launch it.
  • Locate the “MCOOPCASH” app: Use the search box to do so. Key in the name of the application, and wait as the results are being processed.
  • Click on MCOOPCASH icon: This will take you to a window containing the apps details, related applications, and much more.
  • Hit the “Install” button: This is usually green in colour and located just below the name of the app.
  • Accept the permissions: Click on the “Accept” button after authorization. The download will start immediately. You can also use other third-party stores such as apkpure and apkmirror for Mco-op cash app apk download.
  • Launch the MCOOPCASH app: Navigate to your app list, scroll until you trace this application. Click on it to open it.
  • Log in: Use the OTP PIN that was sent to your SIM card and your mobile phone.
  • Change the PIN: Lastly, you have to change your login details to something you can easily remember, yet no one can guess. Do not share this with anyone or write it somewhere.

    How to activate MCo-op cash app on iOS

    App installations on iPhone are done via the Apple Store, which has millions of choices covering games, financing sectors, food ordering and much more. Below is how you can activate the MCoop cash app on iOS:

  • Connect to the internet: To download and install this application, you will need a Wi-Fi or data connection. Make sure you have enough data bundles.
  • Open App Store: This is usually a blue logo with three lines that make a capital A. Tap on it to open it. With numerous recommendations and various categories, this store can be daunting for first-time users. However, we will show you how easy it is to download the MCo-op cash application.
  • Search for the app: Use the search tab, type in the word “Mcoopcash” and hit the enter button. You will be taken to a results page. The chances are that you will locate it.
  • Tap on its icon: You will be taken to a new page that contains screenshots and more details about MCo-op.
  • Hit “Get”: You don’t need any credit or debit cards to download it as it is freely available in the store.
  • Authenticate the installation: Enter your password or use your touch/face ID if you have set it up to verify the installation. The download will begin immediately.
  • Open the app: Immediately after the process is successful, tap the “Open” button from the App Store.
  • Enter your credentials: To activate your account, you will need to key in your phone number and the OTP PIN sent to your SIM card.
  • Change your login details: Immediately after you are logged in using the change password option to use a pin of your choice. Do not share your PIN with anyone.

    How to check Cooperative Bank balance via phone

    Well, now you have successfully been enrolled in the service. You have also downloaded and activated your Mcoop cash app. How do you ascertain how much funds are left on your account using your gadget?

    You will need the following:

    • Active internet connection,
    • Your PIN
    • Some 30 seconds or so from your schedule
    • The phone you installed and activated your Mcoop cash account on

    Use the following step by step guide, assuming you are connected to a reliable source of internet connection.

  • Open MCOOPCASH: Go to your app list, scroll until you see the app. Tap on its icon to open it up.
  • Login: Enter your MCoop PIN. And hit the enter button.
  • Go to the “All Services” tab: This is the first option on the bottom part of the app’s homepage. Tap on it.
  • Select “Check Balance”: Follow prompts to see how many funds are in your account.

    How to transfer money from MCo-op cash to mobile wallet

    Assuming that your app is launched and you have an internet connection, use the procedure below to withdraw any amount to your M-Pesa or Airtel Money wallet:

  • Log in: Enter your mobile banking pin and hit the “Sign in” app.
  • Select mobile money: This option is visible on the homepage screen.
  • Choose between M-Pesa/Airtel Money: If you want to send the money to your Safaricom line, use the M-Pesa option.
  • Enter the mobile number: Key in the receiver’s number and confirm it.
  • Select the account: If you have multiple accounts, you will be prompted to choose the account you wish to withdraw from.
  • Specify the amount: Make sure the amount is correct, and your balance is sufficient to make the transaction.
  • Confirm the transaction: Ascertain whether the details you provided are correct.

    MCoop cash pin retrieval

    In case you forget your PIN, you can reset it by yourself. Alternatively, you can opt to reach out to customer representatives or visit any nearest Co-op Bank branch with necessary documents that prove your identity. Note that you cannot reset your PIN via the MCo-op cash app.

    You will need the following:

    • Active M-Pesa account linked to the number you registered MCo-op cash
    • A minimum of Ksh 1.00 balance on M-Pesa
    • National ID/Passport number you used during registration
    • Airtime balance.

    Use the procedure below:

  • Open your “Phone” app: In some old models, this application is named dial.
  • Key in MCo-op USSD code: Use the following number; *667#
  • Reply with 1: Accept the terms and conditions by keying in 1 again and hitting the “Send” button.
  • Choose between M-Pesa and Call methods: As earlier mentioned, you can reset your PIN by directly calling customer cares via the contacts listed here. If you choose M-Pesa, continue with the procedure.
  • Confirm deposit: An M-Pesa popup will appear prompting you to accept Ksh. 1.00 transfer to your Co-op Bank account.
  • Enter M-Pesa pin: Make sure you enter the correct credentials; otherwise, you will have to repeat this procedure from step 1. A 6-digit password will be sent to you after a successful transaction.
  • Dial *667# again: Enter your MCoop cash pin. Reply with option 1
  • Enter the password sent to you: This code will expire after 30 minutes, so be sure to use it within the stipulated time.
  • Type in a new PIN: Make sure it is memorable and cannot be guessed by your close friends or family members.

    MCo-op cash app loans

    You can get credits of up to Ksh. 500,000 on this app, depending on your CRB score and other factors. To apply, use the procedure below:

  • Connect to the internet: Once done, launch the app and log in using your PIN.
  • Go to Loans: On the all services tab, you will see this option. Click on it and move to the next step.
  • Choose “Apply Loan”: This is the first choice in the next window.
  • Follow prompts: Depending on your loan limit, specify the amount you need.

    • The amount will be credited to your account. A 3% appraisal-fee, 1.083% commission, and 20% exercise duty of the total commission will be deducted from the loan. You can withdraw the amount using any convenient method.

    The loan should be repaid for a maximum period of 6 months. After 30days from the repayment deadline, you will be listed in the CRB. Note that you can only take one loan at a time which cannot be topped up

    To repay the amount, use the steps below, assuming that you are already logged in to the app:

  • Go to loans: The icon is visible on the homepage.
  • Navigate to “Pay Loan”: This is the send option on the menu.
  • Follow prompts.
  • Open your sim toolkit app: Launch your app list and locate it.
  • Choose the preferred SIM card: If you are operating on a dual SIM gadget, select the line you wish to make payments from.
  • Go to “M-Pesa: This will be the first option in the window.
  • Select “Lipa na M-Pesa”: Scroll down and click on the second last choice.
  • Hit “Pay Bill”: Enter 400200 as the paybill number in the next window and hit the OK button.
  • Key in your account number: You will be prompted to enter an account no. Use your Co-op Bank number here.
  • Specify the amount: You can pay half or the wholesome at a go.
  • Enter your PIN: To authenticate the payment, you have to key in your correct security details.

    • If the transaction was successful, you will receive SMSs from M-Pesa and Co-operative Bank with details of the payment.
