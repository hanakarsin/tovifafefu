    JKUAT is one of the leading public Universities in Kenya. It began in 1981 with a small admission of 180 students, but today they have about 35,000 students. JKUAT offers both self-sponsored and regular programmes and offers certificate, diploma, undergraduate, and postgraduate programmes at the main campus and the various centers. JKUAT has also adopted the e-learning and open distance learning approach and has approved the same for its multiple centers. These centers within and outside the country were set up in a bid to increase access to education. The institution located in Juja town in the quiet suburbs of Nairobi along the Thika-Nairobi superhighway. JKuat offers a hub of academic interactions for both local and international students. The institution which is a center of excellence provides tutorial services in the fields of Health Sciences and Applied Sciences, Engineering, Built Environment. Enterprise Development, Agriculture, and Technology. If you are looking to join a higher learning institution in Kenya, then you might want to join JKUAT as it is one of the most preferred by Kenyan employers for when recruiting their workforce.

    Does JKUAT offer diploma courses?

    The answer to this question is yes. There are many diploma courses at Jkuat, all you need to do is choose one that you are interested in and check the requirements, and Jkuat diploma course fee structure after which you will submit your application and wait of feedback. If you meet all the requirements for the JKUAT diploma courses, then you will get admission. This article covers top JKUAT diploma courses offered. The top Diploma courses offered at Jkuat University are in the following departments; Business: Diploma in Business Administration, supplies Management, and Purchasing. Technology: Diploma in Information Technology, Diploma in Business Information Technology, Diploma in Food Technology. Health Science and Applied Sciences: Diploma in Clinical Medicine, Diploma in HIV/AIDS Management. Built Environment: Diploma in Architecture

    Diploma in Architecture

    It is a six-semester programme offered by JKUAT in collaboration with the Nairobi Institute of Technology at the NIT campus located in Westlands, Nairobi. Upon completion of the Diploma in Architecture, if you score distinction or credit, you can apply for the Bachelor of Architecture programme and get two years exemption if you score a pass you can ply to join the first year.

    • One study units from the Group II subjects or Group IV subjects Computer studies, Agriculture, Aviation technology, Art, and design, or home science or Group v subjects French, Arabic, German, Business Studies or Music.
