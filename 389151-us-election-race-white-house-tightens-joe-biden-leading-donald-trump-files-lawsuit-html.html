    US election: Biden overtakes Trump in Georgia and Pennsylvania, inches even closer to victory

    The race to The White House is tightening. None of the two main contenders, President Donald Trump (Republican Party) and Democratic Party candidate Joe Biden, has hit the 270 mark, the minimum number of Electoral College votes a candidate needs to win the presidency.

    16 Mar 2024, 00:00

    The race to The White House is tightening. None of the two main contenders, President Donald Trump (Republican Party) and Democratic Party candidate Joe Biden, has hit the 270 mark, the minimum number of Electoral College votes a candidate needs to win the presidency.

    Trump, who soon after the Tuesday, November 3, poll claimed the vote was rigged in Biden's favour, has already filed lawsuits in places like Georgia.

    According to the president, who is a distance behind Biden, poll workers continued counting mailed-in votes which were presented even after official polling hours.

    Biden, who appears to be on the lead, has called for calm and has expressed confidence that he will ascend to power even as tension continues to mount across the US.

    TUKO.co.ke will provide you with insightful and credible live updates of the election as Americans wait to see who becomes their commander in chief for the next four years.

    Joe Biden receives extra security as his election victory looms

    The United States (US) Secret Service has beefed up security around former vice president Joe Biden as he inches closer to becoming president, according to The Washington Post.

    Nov 06 6:34 PM

    Donald Trump not ready to concede defeat, sources claim

    Sources in The White House have indicated President Donald Trump is not ready to concede even if Joe Biden is declared the winner of the US presidential election, CBS reported.

    Nov 06 6:00 PM

    Joe Biden now leading in Pennsylvania, on the verge of being declared winner

    Democratic Party candidate Joe Biden has now moved ahead in Pennsylvania where President Donald Trump was previously leading.

    If Biden wins Pennsylvania where ballot counting is still underway, then he does not need any of the other states to reach the required minimum of 270 electoral votes.

    Trump, on the other hands, must win Pennsylvania or he will not remain in office.

    Nov 06 5:12 PM

    Election results expected in Pennsylvania soon

    US media reports indicate electoral officials in Pennsylvania may soon release results of mail-in ballots that have been counted so far.

    The result could bring a big change in the presidential race. There are 20 electoral votes up for grab in this state.

    If Joe Biden wins the electoral votes in the state, he would be declared winner of the US presidential election.

    Nov 06 5:04 PM

    Joe Biden continues to lead in race to White House

    According to CNN, preliminary election results show Joe Biden is now leading in Georgia, Nevada, and is projected to lead in Pennsylvania and is now on the verge of becoming the next president of the United States.

    Nov 05 10:33 PM

    Joe Biden says he will be president of all Americans if he wins poll

    Joe Biden says, "Let me be clear: I campaigned as a proud Democrat, but I will govern as an American president".

    Nov 05 10:06 PM

    Popular Vote: Joe Biden leading with over 72.14M votes

    On the popular vote, Biden has amassed 72.14 million votes (50.5%).

    Donald Trump has 68.40 million votes which translate to 47.9%.

    Nov 05 10:01 PM

    Electoral College: Biden-253, Trump-213

    It has been a tight day. Donald Trump, who since Thursday morning has stagnated at, 213 on the electoral college, is trailing behind Joe Biden who is nearing the 270 mark.

    Biden is at 253.

    "Be patient, folks. Votes are being counted, and we feel good about where we are", he said.

    Trump claims that votes that came in after election day will not be counted

    Trump has said, "Any vote that came in after election day will not be counted!"

    Election laws, however, state that "ballots counted after Election Day are legitimate as long as they were cast on or before November 3, as per state-by-state rules".

    Twitter has, however, flagged the post.

    Trump foresees successful court challenge against Joe Biden

    Trump has said, "All of the recent Biden claimed States will be legally challenged by us for voter fraud and state election fraud. Plenty of proof - just check out the media. We will win! America First!".

    Trump insists vote counting should stop

    As Trump's chances of remaining at the White House shrink, he has insisted that the counting of votes needs to stop.

    He has already sued for vote malpractice in Michigan, Pennsylvania and Georgia.

    His armed supporters, however, want the counting to continue.

    Nov 05 7:30 AM

    Donald Trump supporters demonstrate in Arizona

    Section of President Donald Trump's supporters has gathered in numbers outside a tallying station in Phoenix, Arizona where they are protesting the balloting exercise.

    Some of the protesters are armed and claim they have been subjected to "voter suppression".

    Nov 05 3:52 AM

    Donald Trump files lawsuit, insists poll is rigged

    President Donald Trump, whose chances of remaining at The White House are shrinking fast by the minute, has gone to court to contest elections in Georgia and two other places.

    He has claimed poll workers have been counting "dumped" ballots in favour of Biden, thus "disenfranchising" the electorate.

    Trump, who has been trampled upon on the electoral college by Biden, has remained firm that he is the legitimate winner of the election.

    "Our lawyers have asked for “meaningful access”, but what good does that do? The damage has already been done to the integrity of our system, and to the Presidential Election itself. This is what should be discussed!" said Trump.
