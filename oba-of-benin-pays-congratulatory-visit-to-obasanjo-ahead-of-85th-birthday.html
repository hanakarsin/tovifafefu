    01 Jan 0001, 00:00

    The Oba of Benin, Ewuare II has paid a congratulatory visit to Chief Olusegun Obasanjo ahead of his 85th birthday.

    The Oba of Benin who is presently in a state visit to Ogun State hosted by the Alake of Egba Land took it upon himself to visit the Former President.

    He was accompanied by the Chief Host Oba Adedotun Aremu Gbadebo III – The Alake of Egba Land, Oba Saburee Babajide Isola Bakre, the Agura of Gbagura, the President of Abeokuta Club – Chief Tokunbo Odebunmi and the UK High Commissioner, Ambassador Sarafa Tunji Isola.

    Obasanjo will be turning 85 on the 5th of March.

    Mohammed has intention to contest in the presidential election in 2023 under the platform of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP).
