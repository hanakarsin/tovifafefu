    Anambra State Governorship Election LGA/Polling Unit Results

    Naija News reports that the major parties that contested the election include the ruling All Progressives Grand Alliance (APGA), All Progressives Congress (APC), Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) and Young Progressives Party (YPP).

    16 May 2024, 00:00

    The result of the Anambra State Governorship election held across the Southeast state today, Saturday, has started trickling in.

    Naija News reports that the major parties that contested the election include the ruling All Progressives Grand Alliance (APGA), All Progressives Congress (APC), Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) and Young Progressives Party (YPP).

    One of Senator Andy Uba, Valentine Ozigbo, Charles Soludo and Senator Ifeanyi Ubah is expected to be announced as the winner.

    Stay with NaijaNews as we bring you election results…
