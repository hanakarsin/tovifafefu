    Ankara is a term used to refer to native wear made from printed African fabric, mostly cotton. Items from this fabric may include shoes, trousers, dresses, jackets, hats, and shorts. Ankara has been in existence since the 19th century but only gained popularity over the last few decades. While the fabric was typically associated with women's clothing, it has now become accepted across all genders. What are some of the most fashionable Ankara styles today? Akinyemi Barakat, Content Creator and Social Media Manager at Losode Inc., shares her expertise on popular Ankara styles for men.

    Tips for incorporating Ankara into your wardrobe

    Akinyemi also provided some valuable advice for men who are new to incorporating Ankara outfits into their wardrobe:

  • Start Small with Simple Pieces: To get comfortable with vibrant patterns, begin with basic items like shirts or accessories.
  • Use Less Patterned Ankara Fabrics: Opt for fabrics with subtler patterns to avoid overwhelming your look.
  • Go for Neutral Colors: Choose Ankara fabrics in neutral tones for a more versatile and understated approach.
  • Choose the Right Style: Ensure the style of the garment suits your body type and personal fashion sense.
  • Mix and match: Combine Ankara pieces with plain clothing items to balance your outfit.
  • Layer it Well: Use Ankara as a layering piece, like a blazer or jacket, to add a pop of color and pattern without making it overpowering.
