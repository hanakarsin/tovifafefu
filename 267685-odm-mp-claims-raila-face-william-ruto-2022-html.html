    ODM MP claims Raila will face William Ruto in 2022

    Budalangi Member of Parliament, Raphael Wanjala has condemned claims by former Kakamega Senator, Bonni Khalwale that National Super Alliance (NASA) coalition was going to pick another presidential candidate come 2022.

    Wanjala argued the voters who vote for the coalition principal, Raila Odinga , are the only ones who will decide when Raila should stop vying for presidency.

    The MP was speaking to journalist when he revealed that the opposition coalition was formed in order for Raila Odinga to take over the government.

    He added that since the coalitions missions had not been accomplished, Raila will continue to run for presidency until his supporters ask him to stop

    'When NASA was formed it was was formed that we take over the government, NASA did not takeover the government, therefore Raila is our party leader and we have our candidate in the name of Raila 2022." he added.

    "so if they have any another other candidate let them bring but we have our candidate," he said.

    Wanjala's remarks came after Khalwalwe announced on Live tv during an interview that the coalition was going to announce another presidential candidate to face Deputy President William Ruto come 2022.
