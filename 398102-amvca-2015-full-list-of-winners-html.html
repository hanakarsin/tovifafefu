    The 2015 edition of Africa Magic Viewersâ€™ Choice Awards finished moments ago at the Eko Hotel Convention Centre, Lagos.

    The competition for the prestigious award was as tight as never before. The list of nominees proves it.

    The Nigerian psychological thriller, October 1, and comedy move The Meeting, starring Rita Dominic, Femi Jacobs were among the favourites.

    Let's see if their expectations turned into reality.

    AMVCA 2015 list of winners is given below:

    1. Best Actor in a Comedy - Femi Jacobs - The Meeting

    2. Best Actress in a Comedy - Rita Dominic - The Meeting

    3. Best Actor in a Drama - OC Ukeje - Confusion Na Wa

    4. Best Actress in a Drama - Kehinde Bankole - October 1

    5. Best Art Director (Movie/ TV) - Pat Nebo - October 1

    6. Best Cinematographer (Movie/ TV) - Stanlee Ohikhuare - Verdict

    7. Best Comedy Writer (Movie/ TV) - Tunde Babalola - The Meeting

    8. Best Costume Designer (Movie/ TV) - Deola Sagoe & Obijie Oru - October 1

    9. Best Documentary - Yvonne Bassey - The Gift of The Nile

    10. Best Drama Writer (Movie/ TV) - Tunde Babalola - October 1

    11. Best Lighting Designer - Lanre Omofaiye - October 1

    12. Best Local Language Hausa - Hafizu Bello & Abubakar Bashir - Bincike

    13. Best Local Language Igbo - Obi Emelonye - Onye Ozi (The Messenger)

    14. Best Local Language Swahili - Sarika Hemi Lakhani - Veve

    15. Best Local Language Yoruba - Fathia Balogun - Iya Alalake

    16. Best Make Up Artist - Sacred Lola Maja - October 1

    17. Best Movie (Comedy) - Mildred Okwo & Rita Dominic - The Meeting

    18. Best Movie (Drama) - Steve Gukas - A Place in The Stars

    19. Best Movie Director - Kunle Afolayan - October 1

    20. Best New Media Online Video - Imoh Umoren - Hard Times

    21. Best Movie of 2014 - Kunle Afolayan - October 1

    22. Best Short Film - Ekene Mekwunye - Oblivious

    23. Best Sound Editing - Kulanen Ikyo - October 1

    24. Best Video Editor - Victoria Akujobi - Reflections

    25. Best Supporting Actress - Linda Ejiofor - The Meeting

    26. Best Supporting Actor - Blossom Chukwujekwu - Knocking on Heavens Door

    27. Best Television Series - Georgia Arnold - Shuga
